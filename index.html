To ensure a better future for polar bears, we must cut down on greenhouse emissions. There are many ways to do that.

Such as:

  • USE LED LIGHTBULBS, They use up to 80% less energy than conventional incandescents
  • REDUCE REUSE RECYCLE, Cut down on the amount of waste you throw away. It conserves natural resources, landfill space and energy
